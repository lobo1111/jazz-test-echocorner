# Relationship Summary

The relationships below highlight the business connections that make the vocabulary in this chapter useful across the dossier.

| Concept A | Relationship | Concept B | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Operator` | governs participation of | `Administrator company` | The operator controls which administrator companies may serve in the governed portfolio. |
| `Operator` | authorizes or oversees | `Administrator assignment` | Assignment and reassignment are governed business decisions, not informal changes. |
| `Administrator assignment` | makes responsible for | `Community` | The assignment is the business relationship that connects one active administrator company to one community at a time. |
| `Administrator company` | administers during active assignment | `Community` | The administrator company's accountability exists only within the period of assignment. |
| `Owner` | holds | `Estate / unit` | Ownership of an estate or unit is the business anchor of standing. |
| `Estate / unit` | links owner to | `Community` | The property relationship explains why a given owner is relevant to a given community. |
| `Community membership eligibility` | recognizes standing of | `Owner` | Eligibility confirms whether the owner may participate in a specific community context. |
| `Community membership eligibility` | applies within | `Community` | Participation rights are tied to one community, not to general platform membership. |
| `Administrator assignment` | authorizes publication of | `Community wall post` | Community communication is accountable to the administrator company responsible at that time. |
| `Owner` | raises | `Support ticket` | A support matter enters the business model through an owner with recognized standing. |
| `Support ticket` | is handled by active | `Administrator company` | Support responsibility follows the community's current assignment. |
| `Community wall post` | becomes part of | `Communication record` | Important community communications remain part of the durable business history. |
| `Support ticket` | becomes part of | `Communication record` | Support handling creates evidence of accountability, progress, and outcome. |
| `Communication record` | preserves history across | `Administrator assignment` | Historical communication and support evidence should survive administrator change. |

## Relationship Pattern Notes

- The most important pattern is the chain from `Owner` to `Estate / unit` to `Community membership eligibility` to community-specific participation.
- The second critical pattern is the chain from `Operator` to `Administrator assignment` to `Administrator company` accountability for a `Community`.
- `Communication record` is not a separate operational stream. It is the continuity layer that preserves the meaning of communication and support when time, responsibility, or interpretation changes.
