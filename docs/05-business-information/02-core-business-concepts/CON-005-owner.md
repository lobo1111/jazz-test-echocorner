# Owner

## Definition

An **Owner** is the person whose standing within a community arises through ownership of one or more estates or units linked to that community.

## Why It Matters

The owner is the main participating business party on the community side of the domain. Communication rights, commenting rights, and support interactions all depend on the owner's legitimate standing rather than on general registration in a platform.

## Key Characteristics

- may hold one or more `Estate / unit` relationships
- may participate in more than one `Community`
- gains participation rights through `Community membership eligibility`
- may receive community communications and raise community-relevant support matters

## Relationships To Other Concepts

- holds an `Estate / unit`
- gains `Community membership eligibility` for a specific `Community`
- consumes or participates in `Community wall post` communication where entitled
- raises a `Support ticket` to the responsible `Administrator company`
- contributes to the `Communication record` when participating in communication or support interactions

## Lifecycle States

The owner as a person is stable. What changes in business terms is whether the owner currently has standing within a specific community through active ownership and confirmed eligibility.

## Business Owner Or Steward

There is no single steward of the owner as a business person. The active `Administrator company` typically interprets and maintains the owner's community-specific standing for communication and support purposes.

## Synonyms Or Alternate Names

- property owner
- community owner

## Rules And Constraints

- owner participation is community-specific, not a general right across all communities
- one owner may legitimately appear in multiple communities through multiple holdings
- the exact boundary between owner participation and broader resident interaction remains unresolved
