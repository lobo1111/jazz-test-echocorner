# Overview

**Housing Community Administration and Resident Communication in Poland** is the business domain in which EchoCorner is being framed. The domain brings together the administration of residential communities, the governance of administrator companies that serve those communities, and the communication channels through which owners receive information and raise matters requiring attention.

The domain matters because housing communities depend on clear day-to-day administration, timely communication, and accountable handling of owner concerns. In practice, these needs span several parties at once: an **Operator** that governs participation in the platform, **Administrator companies** that manage portfolios of communities, individual **Communities** that require continuity and accountability, and **Owners** whose rights and obligations arise from owned **Estates / units**.

The dossier treats these concerns as one connected business story rather than as separate topics. A community must have exactly one active **Administrator company** at a time, owners may participate in more than one community through their holdings, and changes in administrator responsibility must preserve continuity of responsibility and historical records.

This chapter gives the reader the business frame needed to understand the rest of the dossier: what this operating area is, why it deserves dedicated attention, and which business boundaries shape the work that follows.
