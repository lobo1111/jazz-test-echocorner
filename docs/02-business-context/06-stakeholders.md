# Stakeholders

## Operator

The **Operator** is the portfolio-governance party for the domain. Its interests are in controlling which **Administrator companies** participate, assigning and reassigning community responsibility in a governed way, and maintaining confidence that the operating model remains accountable and traceable across communities.

## Administrator Company

An **Administrator company** is the organization responsible for managing one or more **Communities**. Its interests are in clear assignment of responsibility, manageable communication obligations, orderly handling of owner support matters, and continuity when communities enter or leave its portfolio.

## Community

A **Community** is the housing-community entity whose administration, communication, and support interactions are being governed. Its interests are in continuity of administration, clear accountability, relevant communication to participating owners, and preservation of business records when administrator responsibility changes.

## Owner

An **Owner** is the person whose participation in a **Community** arises through ownership of one or more **Estates / units**. The owner's interests are in receiving relevant information, understanding when participation is permitted, raising matters to the responsible administrator company, and seeing that issues affecting the community are handled responsibly.

## Estate / Unit

An **Estate / unit** is the owned property through which an **Owner** has standing within a **Community**. Its stakeholder significance is that it anchors participation rights, communication eligibility, and the relationship between an owner and the communities in which that owner has a legitimate interest.

## Administrator Assignment

An **Administrator assignment** is the time-bound business relationship linking a **Community** to its active **Administrator company**. It matters because responsibility, communication authority, support handling, and historical continuity all depend on this relationship being clear and governable.
