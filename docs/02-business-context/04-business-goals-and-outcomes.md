# Business Goals And Outcomes

## Business Goals

The business goals for this domain are:

- establish a clear and governable relationship between each **Community** and its active **Administrator company**
- ensure that owners receive relevant community-specific information through recognized communication channels
- enable owners to raise support or maintenance matters to the responsible administrator company with clear business accountability
- allow the **Operator** to onboard administrator companies and oversee assignment and reassignment across communities
- preserve the business records and historical continuity needed for operational confidence, auditability, and legal defensibility

## Expected Business Outcomes

If the initiative succeeds, the business should see the following outcomes:

- each community has one active **Administrator assignment** at any point in time, with prior assignments preserved as part of the business record
- owners understand which community communications are relevant to them and when they are permitted to participate
- administrator companies can manage communication and support obligations across multiple communities without ambiguity about responsibility
- community transitions between administrator companies can be governed without losing essential business continuity
- the dossier can support later capability, behavior, and vocabulary work without repeated redefinition of scope or terms

## Direction Of Improvement

The intended direction of change is toward greater accountability, clearer participation boundaries, improved continuity during reassignment, and more reliable handling of owner-facing communication and support matters. These goals describe the business result to be achieved, not the means by which it will later be implemented.
