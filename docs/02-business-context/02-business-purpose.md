# Business Purpose

## Primary Business Purpose

The primary business purpose of this domain is to support accountable administration of housing communities in Poland while providing owners with controlled, community-specific channels for information, discussion, and support requests. The domain exists to make responsibilities, participation rights, and service continuity clearer across the relationship between the **Operator**, **Administrator companies**, **Communities**, and **Owners**.

## Secondary Business Purposes

Secondary purposes include:

- enabling portfolio-level oversight of which **Administrator company** is responsible for each **Community**
- preserving business continuity when a community changes from one administrator company to another
- improving the reliability and traceability of communication that affects owners and communities
- giving owners a recognized route to raise maintenance or support matters to the responsible administrator company
- establishing a business foundation that can absorb Polish legal or regulatory obligations without redefining the core domain each time

## Purpose Versus Means

The purpose of the initiative is business clarity, accountability, and controlled participation. It is not to prescribe a particular operating tool, interaction model, or organizational design. Decisions about software structure, process automation, or technical controls may later support this purpose, but they are not the purpose itself and are outside the scope of this chapter.
