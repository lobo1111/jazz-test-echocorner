# UC-001: Publish Community Announcement

## Goal / Outcome

An official community communication is issued by the currently responsible **Administrator company** to the right **Owner** audience for a specific **Community**, with enough business traceability to support later review.

## Primary Business Actor

**Administrator company**

## Other Participating Actors

- **Owner**
- **Community**

## Related Capabilities

- Primary: `Community announcement management`
- Supporting: `Owner eligibility management`
- Supporting: `Business record stewardship`

## Trigger

The **Administrator company** needs to communicate a community-relevant notice, update, disruption, reminder, or decision-related message to owners of a specific **Community**.

## Preconditions

- The **Community** has one active **Administrator assignment**.
- The publishing **Administrator company** is the one currently responsible for that **Community**.
- The communication topic falls within the business responsibilities of the current administrator relationship.
- The intended owner audience can be determined through community-specific standing linked to an **Estate / unit**.

## Postconditions

- The announcement exists as an official community communication for the relevant audience.
- The business can show which **Community** the announcement concerns and which **Administrator company** issued it.
- A durable **Communication record** exists for accountability and later continuity needs.

## Main Success Flow

1. The **Administrator company** identifies a business need to communicate with owners of a specific **Community**.
2. The administrator confirms that the topic belongs within its current responsibility for that community.
3. The administrator determines the owner audience entitled to receive the communication.
4. The administrator frames the announcement as an official community communication with a clear business purpose.
5. The announcement is issued to the eligible owner audience for that **Community**.
6. The communication is preserved as part of the ongoing business record for the community.

## Alternate Flows

- The announcement is urgent, so the **Administrator company** communicates the essential business message first and completes supporting detail when the facts stabilize.
- The communication applies only to a subset of owners within the **Community**, so the audience is narrowed according to the relevant **Estate / unit** standing.
- The announcement is informational only and does not invite discussion, even though it still remains part of the official community communication record.

## Exception Flows

- The responsible **Administrator company** is unclear because reassignment timing is disputed, so the announcement cannot proceed until accountability is clarified.
- The communication topic falls outside the administrator's business remit, so the announcement is withheld or redirected to the proper governing party.
- The communication may carry formal legal significance beyond the currently confirmed scope, so the matter is flagged for business clarification before issue.

## Business Rules Involved

- A **Community** must have exactly one active **Administrator company** at a time.
- Official community communication must be tied to the currently active **Administrator assignment**.
- Owner access to community communication is community-specific rather than platform-wide.
- Announcement history must remain part of the business record even if the active administrator relationship later changes.

## Information Used

- Community identity and current **Administrator assignment**
- Owner standing through **Estate / unit**
- Announcement purpose, scope, and relevance
- Prior related **Communication records**

## KPIs Or Measures Affected

- Timeliness of official community communication
- Share of announcements issued to the correct owner audience
- Completeness of communication records retained for later review

## Notes And Assumptions

- This use case describes business publication authority, not channel mechanics or presentation format.
- The dossier does not yet decide which communications are merely operational and which may carry formal legal significance.
