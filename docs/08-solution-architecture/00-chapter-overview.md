# Chapter Overview

This chapter establishes the solution architecture foundation for EchoCorner for the domain of **Housing Community Administration and Resident Communication in Poland**.

It extends the merged business dossier by translating business drivers, capabilities, behavior, and vocabulary into stable architectural boundaries, principles, and modeling decisions. It does not replace the business dossier, and it does not introduce container design, APIs, data schemas, or implementation technology choices.

The chapter creates the frame that later architecture chapters must inherit:

- Chapter 09 should elaborate strategic domain ownership and context internals without changing the bounded-context seams set here.
- Chapter 10 should elaborate cross-context and external interaction patterns without redefining business accountability or record continuity.
- Chapter 11 should elaborate internal application structure without changing the glossary, C4 scope, or core lifecycle invariants established here.

Read this chapter after the merged business dossier in `docs/01-front-matter/` through `docs/06-cross-artifact-alignment/`. Use it as the architectural bridge between business analysis and later architecture specialization.
